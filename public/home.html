What does the subject HCI stand for?
Niko:
HCI stands for Human-Computer Interaction. It's a field of study that focuses on the design and evaluation of computer technology, specifically on the interaction between humans (the users) and computers. HCI professionals aim to make technology more user-friendly, efficient, and enjoyable.
